PROTOCOL WHITEPAPER v1.0.4

1. Executive Summary

Sentinel Protocol is a decentralized, multi-chain custody solution designed to solve the "Digital Black Hole" problem. As of 2026, over 4.5 million BTC are lost due to death or lost keys. We provide a fail-safe mechanism for asset inheritance.

2. Multi-Chain Security Architecture

We deploy on Ethereum (L1) for ultimate security, BNB Chain for accessibility, and Polygon for scalable vault interactions. Every piece of data is fragmented via Shamir's Secret Sharing (SSS).

3. The Vault Sharding Mechanism

When a user deposits a key or document into the Sentinel Vault, it is encrypted with AES-256. The resulting cipher is sharded into 10 pieces. These pieces are distributed across decentralized IPFS nodes. No single entity, including Sentinel, can reconstruct the data.
